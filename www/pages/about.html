Glåüm Interactivity Description

We in the Glåüm community value connection and creative expression. Our goal is to provide a space where people can relax in an environment that encourages genuine connections.
At Glåüm, we balance relaxation with opportunities for fun and meaningful interaction. Whether you're looking for a quiet space or a lively celebration, we have something for everyone.

Our space at Burning Man includes a 26ft geodesic dome, filled with diffused lights, cushions, music, and incredible people. We host quiet performances and readings during the day, offering a peaceful escape from the outside world.

We are dedicated to radical inclusion and strive to make Glåüm a welcoming environment for everyone. We encourage participation in our Glåüm initiation ceremony, held at the end of the week, followed by a celebratory dance party.



The Word of Glåüm

In today's complex world, it can be difficult to find meaning and direction. At Glåüm, we believe in helping others find their way through connection, creativity, and expression. We welcome you to join our community and discover the power of Glåüm.
